Tradition of Gifting

Despite the fact that one has to go thru difficult fast, women eagerly wait for festival.One reason for this joy and

happiness is a golden opportunity to get expensive gifts and opportunity to get dressed up as bride. Since this festival is considered very important for

married women, all symbols, which reflect the state of marriage a woman become high in

high demand, such as jewelry, bangles, henna, sarees, lehnga choli, and many

other traditional gifts.
